At Mr. Coffee, our commitment begins in coffee.

We could not offer a space for freedom of expression and sharing without thinking about what we serve in our cups.

All our coffees come from fair trade farms: farmers receive fair remuneration for their labor, working conditions respect human rights.

This is what makes our coffees so good: they grow, are grown, picked, and dried with respect and love.

Mr. Coffee, a coffee (almost) like any other.

Here, we come to find someone, or to get together, to work on our computer or relax, to read among the works in our library or simply to enjoy a good coffee.

All this world so different that crosses without seeing each other, that we made you want to do something more.

This is how Mr. Coffee, the philosopher, was born.

Our ambition: to bring together people from all walks of life around social issues, philosophical debates, their common passion for writing and letters.
